## Introduction

Prompt engineering has evolved from an art into a discipline with measurable outcomes and reproducible techniques.

## Structured Output Formatting

One of the most impactful techniques is explicitly requesting structured output formats like JSON or XML from the model.

### Schema Definition

Providing a clear schema in your prompt significantly reduces parsing errors and hallucinated fields.

## Chain of Thought Prompting

Encouraging the model to reason step-by-step before arriving at an answer dramatically improves accuracy on complex tasks.

## Few-Shot Examples

Including 2-3 carefully chosen examples in your prompt helps the model understand the desired output format and quality level.

## Temperature and Sampling

Understanding how temperature affects output quality is crucial for production systems where consistency matters.

## Conclusion

Effective prompt engineering is about reducing ambiguity and providing clear constraints that guide the model toward your desired output.
